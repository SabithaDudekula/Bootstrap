BIRYANI MANIA

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with succulent pieces of chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of spices such as cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and bay leaves. It is often layered with fried onions, fresh herbs like mint and coriander, and cooked slowly to allow the flavors to infuse. Traditionally served with raita or salad, chicken biryani is a beloved dish in South Asian cuisine.

Mutton Biryani

Mutton biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with tender, slow-cooked mutton and aromatic spices. It blends basmati rice with rich masalas, creating a perfect balance of taste and aroma. Often served with raita or salan, it’s a royal treat for special occasions.

Prawns Biryani

Prawns Biryani is a delicious seafood delight made with juicy prawns, aromatic basmati rice, and a blend of flavorful spices. The prawns are cooked until tender, then layered with rice, herbs, and fried onions for a rich taste. Served hot with raita or salad, it’s a perfect treat for seafood lovers.

Fish Biryani

Fish Biryani is a mouthwatering dish made with tender fish pieces, fragrant basmati rice, and a mix of aromatic spices. The fish is marinated and cooked gently to keep it soft and flavorful, then layered with rice, fried onions, and fresh herbs. Served with raita or curry, it’s a unique and tasty twist on traditional biryani.
